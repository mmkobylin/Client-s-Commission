Jeremy Walton

Artist

Process

Inspired by Abstract Expressionist artists such as Helen Frankenthaler, but with an interest in the visual phenomenon of paraedolia, the paintings are made up of carefully rendered layers, gradually building towards the final work.

Each layer consists of specific colour selections and dillutions, applied with the overall composition in mind. The result is a level of subtle depth designed to inspire the imagination of the viewer.
